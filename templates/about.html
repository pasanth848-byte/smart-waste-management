Project Overview

The AI Waste Classification System is a web-based application that automatically identifies different types of waste using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN). Users simply upload an image, and the system predicts the waste category while providing the appropriate disposal recommendation.

Problem Statement

Why This Project?

Improper waste segregation is one of the major environmental problems. Many people are unable to identify recyclable and non-recyclable waste correctly, leading to contamination, resource wastage and increased landfill pollution. Our AI-based waste classification system addresses this issue by automatically identifying waste categories from images and providing proper disposal recommendations.
